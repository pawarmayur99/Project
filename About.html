Our people

At CareerLinkup, our mission is to help people get jobs. We have more than 14,600 global employees passionately pursuing this purpose and improving the recruitment journey through real stories and data. We foster a collaborative workplace that strives to create the best experience for job seekers.

Our Leadership

CareerLinkup's leadership team is diverse, dedicated and committed to empowering our employees to fulfill our mission: We help people get jobs. By fostering strong partnerships and collaboration, they serve and support job seekers, employers, society and our employees.
